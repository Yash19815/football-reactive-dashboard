## USAGE NOTES

1. **Import Order:** Load tables in this order for referential integrity:
   - seasons → leagues → teams
   - players
   - team_seasons (requires seasons, leagues, teams)
   - player_team_seasons (requires players, teams, seasons)
   - matches (requires seasons, leagues, teams)
   - player_match_stats (requires players, matches, teams)

2. **Data Relationships:**
   - Each team belongs to one league
   - Each team has stats for each season
   - Each player belongs to one team per season
   - Each match involves two teams in one league
   - Each player_match_stats record links a player to a specific match

3. **Statistical Correlations:**
   - xG correlates with actual goals (R² ≈ 0.8)
   - Shot accuracy correlates with player ability
   - Pass accuracy varies by position (GK: 77-82%, DEF: 80-85%, MID: 78-85%, FWD: 65-75%)
   - Player ratings reflect performance across all metrics

4. **Position-Based Aggregations:**
   - Goalkeepers: Focus on saves, distribution, positioning
   - Defenders: Tackles, interceptions, clearances, aerial duels
   - Midfielders: Pass accuracy, key passes, tackles, distance covered
   - Forwards: Goals, shots, dribbles, key passes
